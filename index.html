    School Supplies for Kids

    Organized by RIHAN. Join us in empowering young minds by ensuring every child has the tools they need to succeed in school. Your support will help provide essential school supplies to children in underserved communities, giving them the opportunity to focus on their education and build a brighter future.

    Tree Planting Campaign

    Organized by Green Earth. Take part in our mission to restore the environment by planting trees in urban and rural areas. Your contribution will help create greener communities, reduce carbon emissions, and ensure a healthier planet for future generations.

    Scholarships for Students

    Organized by Bright Future. Invest in the next generation of leaders by supporting scholarships for students from disadvantaged backgrounds. Your donation will provide financial aid to deserving students, helping them achieve their educational dreams and break the cycle of poverty.
